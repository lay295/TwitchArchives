About Us

This is a side project I made for fun to archive my favorite Twitch streamers broadcasts. I plan to expand it to archive popular streamers and possibly expand it into a service where others can archive their own favorite streamers. 

This website is a work in progress, and I hope to expand the functionality and the amount of streamers I archive. The chat replays are rendered using a version of my program on GitHub.

If you are the owner of one of the Twitch channels I am archiving and wish for a video to be removed, or your channel to stop being archived entirely I'm more than happy to do so. Please email admin@twitcharchives.com or DM iLoveKeepo69 on Twitch.

To run this whole thing I use an Unlimited GSuite account that actually costs me $12/m, and not accounting for power and computation costs for the website I don't make any money off of it. If this website has helped you make a YouTube video, consider buying me a coffee ❤️.
